Physical Therapy


Improper joint alignment occurs during our daily activities resulting in joint pain!

At Healthy Arts Physical Therapy, our clients learn to correct alignment problems related to their pain.
 

Our bodies function as a whole; therefore the entire body must be evaluated and treated.

Painful places in the shoulder, neck, back, or knee are often related to dysfunction in other areas of the body.


At Healthy Arts Physical Therapy, most insurance plans are welcomed (including BCBS, United Healthcare, and Medicare). Our staff will check your insurance coverage and let you know the amount of your co-pay. Ask your doctor for a physical thearpy referral.
