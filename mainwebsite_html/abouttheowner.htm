About the Owner

 


David Crenshaw is a licensed Physical Therapist with nineteen years of experience. With certifications in Pilates and Yoga instruction, he rounds out his philosophy of whole body treatment for individuals he serves.

David has worked with patient populations in diverse places, including the South Pacific Island of American Samoa, and the desert Southwest Navajo Reservation. For the past nine years he has directed physical therapy programs in central Illinois.

Graduating in 1986 from the Chicago Medical School, he values the need to refine his skills with continuing education in the latest treatment practices. His interests include manual therapies of joint mobilization, cranio-sacral therapy, myofascaial release and postural retraining.

David has come to understand the power of joining the disciplines of Pilates, Yoga and Tai Chi with a carefully crafted individual Physical Therapy program to maintain ongoing physical well-being.

Originally from Washington, Illinois, David now lives in Eureka with his wife and three children.
