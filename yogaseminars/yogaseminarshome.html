David Crenshaw PT, CPI, CYT, RYT, blends his knowledge of Physical Therapy, Yoga, Pilates and Yoga Therapy in seminars for yogis and yoga teachers.


Half-day and full-day classes are offered.


Currently there are three courses to choose from:

Anatomy of Yoga - An introduction to the major joints of the body, their optimal alignment and common joint problems encountered.

5 Ways to Use Core Muscles in Your Yoga Practice - how core muscles can keep the spine in healthy alignment throughout your yoga practice.

Anatomy of Breathing for Yoga - Understanding how the anatomy and practice of yogic breathing compliments a yoga practice. David is a faculty member of Professional Yoga Therapy Studies. PYTS offers yoga therapy classes and a yoga therapy certification for licensed health care professionals nation wide: www.proyogatherapy.com
